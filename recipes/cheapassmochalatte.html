Cheap Ass Mocha Latte

Recipe

When I was in high school, all the cool kids in my class were drinking coffee. I thought coffee was disgusting, plus the caffeine made me feel sick. Then I started going to coffee shops and discovered the decaf mocha latte. I thought it was genius; I get to drink something chocolate-y, don't have to taste the coffee, and I get to tell my friends that I drink coffee. When my mom learned that I was going to coffee shops, she was determined to help me save money. My mom was very frugal, and refused to pay 3 whole dollars for something she could recreate at home. I think you can already see where this recipe is going.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk of your choice: whole, almond, oat, soy, etc
  • 1 packet of your favorite hot cocoa mix
  • 1 cup drip coffee, with caffeine or decaf

Directions

  1. Microwave milk until hot.
  2. Mix entire contents of hot cocoa packet into milk.
  3. Stir hot cocoa/milk mixture into the cup of coffee.
  4. Stir well, or stir in an ice cube, until cool enough to drink
  5. Microwave cocoa/coffee mixture if you cooled it too much.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until your mixture is drinkable.
  7. Brag to your friends about how you drink coffee now.
